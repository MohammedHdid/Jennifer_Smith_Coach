# Strategy Map: Jennifer Smith Coaching

## 1. Niche & Target Audience
**Brand Name**: Jennifer Smith Coaching
**Target Audience**: Women seeking a structured, supportive fitness coaching program to achieve a "sportif" and toned shape.
**Niche**: Premium Women's Online Fitness Coaching

## 2. Core Objective
**Objective**: Generate Level 1 leads (WhatsApp contact / direct booking inquiries) using a high-converting, single-page static landing page.

## 3. The "Unicorn" Aesthetic
- **Colors**: Deep, premium background (e.g., #0F172A Dark Slate) with vibrant, feminine but strong accents (e.g., #EC4899 Rose Pink or #10B981 Emerald Green).
- **Typography**: Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans. Bold, confident hero headers.
- **Visuals**: High-end lifestyle and fitness photography, showcasing the physical transformation and confidence. Glassmorphism effect on the offer cards.
- **Motion**: Subtle CSS animations for the offer cards on hover.

## 4. Problem-Agitate-Solve (PAS) Framework
- **Problem**: You are consuming free fitness content online but not seeing a toned, athletic shape.
- **Agitate**: Random workouts and restrictive diets are burning you out. You lack a structured plan that actually works for your lifestyle.
- **Solve**: Jennifer Smith's proven coaching system provides the exact roadmap, nutrition, and accountability to transform your body sustainably.

## 5. Risk Reversals & Trust
- **Uncertainty: "Will this work for my body?"** -> **Solution**: Show high-quality imagery and explicit promises of a customized plan.
- **Uncertainty: "How do I start?"** -> **Solution**: Clear, frictionless WhatsApp / Direct CTA buttons.
